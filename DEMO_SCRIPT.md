# 🎬 MindSpace - Quick Demo Script (5 Minutes)

## 🚀 Setup (Before Demo)
1. Run: `python app.py`
2. Open: `http://localhost:5000` in incognito
3. Clear any existing data
4. Have backup tab ready
5. Close other tabs
6. Zoom to 100%

---

## 📝 Demo Flow

### 1️⃣ Landing Page (30 seconds)
**Say:** "This is MindSpace - an AI mental wellness companion."

**Show:**
- Modern gradient hero design
- Feature cards with icons
- Clean, professional UI

**Point out:**
- "Notice the modern gradient design - not clinical"
- "24/7 available, 100% private, free forever"

---

### 2️⃣ Sign Up (30 seconds)
**Do:**
1. Click "Get Started"
2. Show modal (point out 440px width)
3. Click "Anonymous" tab
4. Click "Continue Anonymously"

**Say:** "Privacy-first - no email required if you want anonymity"

---

### 3️⃣ AI Chat (90 seconds)
**Do:**
1. Type: "I'm feeling anxious about my presentation"
2. Wait for AI response
3. Show session sidebar
4. Point to streak tracker
5. Point to crisis support

**Say:** 
- "Real-time AI support with empathetic responses"
- "All conversations saved in sessions"
- "Crisis support always visible"

---

### 4️⃣ Mood Tracking (60 seconds)
**Do:**
1. Click "Mood" in nav
2. Click "Anxious" emoji (😰)
3. Type note: "Nervous about hackathon demo"
4. Click "Save Mood Entry"
5. Show statistics dashboard
6. Scroll to mood history

**Say:**
- "Visual mood tracking with emojis"
- "Track patterns over time"
- "Statistics show your progress"

---

### 5️⃣ Resources - INTERACTIVE TOOLS (90 seconds)

**Do:**
1. Click "Resources" in nav
2. Show crisis banner at top
3. Click "🧘 Exercises" filter
4. Click "Box Breathing Technique"

**Say:** "Watch this - fully interactive breathing exercise"

**Demo the breathing:**
- Show animated box
- Point out countdown timer
- Let it run for 2 cycles
- Click "Stop"

**Then:**
5. Click "🛠️ Tools" filter
6. Click "Thought Journal"
7. Show the CBT form

**Say:** 
- "Interactive CBT tools"
- "Gratitude journal"
- "Panic attack first aid"
- "All fully functional"

**Finally:**
8. Scroll to bottom
9. Show professional help section

**Say:** "Links to real therapist directories and support groups"

---

### 6️⃣ Responsive (15 seconds)
**Do:**
1. Resize browser window
2. Show mobile view

**Say:** "Fully responsive on all devices"
